Ad Sizes

Directory


Half-page ad size facing the shopper as you push the cart.

Header


1/4 page ad size facing the shopper as you push the cart.

Front


Full-page ad size facing shoppers walking towards you.

Directory

About: Facing the shopper as you push the cart there is a half page ad that's called the Directory Ad.

Dimensions: 5.5”w x 7.8”h

Exposure: 20% to 100% Coverage in increments of 20%

Header

About: Facing the shopper as you push the cart there is a 1/4 page ad to the upper right-hand corner.

Dimensions: 4.5”w x 2.5”h

Exposure: 50% or 100% Coverage. Only two possible advertisers per store.

Front

About: The opposite side of the frame, facing shoppers walking towards you, is a full-page ad called the Front Ad.

Dimensions: 11.25”w x 8.25”h

Exposure: 20% to 100% Coverage in increments of 20%

How Ads Are Offered

Ads are offered in 20% increments of the carts. This means that your ad can appear on 20% of all the carts all the way up to 100% of the carts.

Store Data

There are an average of ~25,000 residents who shop at this supermarket each week. It is the one place that people must go multiple times a week and shoppers are shopping 3-4 times a week for about 30-45 minutes each time. This is called forced exposure because you can't go anywhere in the store without running into a cart that is displaying your ad. Your ad will get seen on average of 15-25 times every time a shopper shops! There is a saying that "repetition is king in marketing" and our program certainly exemplifies this. We can put you in front of more people, for a longer period of time and at a lower cost than any other type of marketing near our price point.
